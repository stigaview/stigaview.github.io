SRG-OS-000424 Controls

STIG ID Version Title Product
SRG-OS-000424-GPOS-00188 V2R5 The operating system must implement cryptographic mechanisms to prevent unauthorized disclosure of information and/or detect changes to information during transmission unless otherwise protected by alternative physical safeguards, such as, at a minimum, a Protected Distribution System (PDS).
